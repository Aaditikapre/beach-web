OUR HOTELS AND EVENTS:

Bolongo Bay is a boutique Caribbean property with its own 1,000-foot-long beach lined with palm trees and frequented by iguanas. Enjoy the beachfront pool and the complimentary use of paddleboards, Hobie Cats, kayaks, aqua tricycles, snorkeling gear, and other watersports equipment.

Book Now: bolongo bay

This private island is capped at 60 guests who take up temporary residence in two-story beach houses on stilts. Each of the 15 suites features a king-size bed with mosquito nets, a balcony overlooking the palm-fringed beach, an ensuite bathroom, a wooden-decked lounge area, and daybeds. The resort also has two swimming pools, three bars, a restaurant, acres of virgin rainforest to discover, and coral reefs to snorkel through.

Book Now: Nikoi

This island resort whose name in Sanskrit means "a little patch of heaven" is located within a pristine tropical landscape. If you're looking to escape beaches crowded with tourists and reconnect with the natural world, look no further. Accommodations include villas, suites, and hotel rooms. Several onsite dining options are available.

Book Now: pimilai

Bounded by the Great Barrier Reef and the Wet Tropics rainforest, the luxurious Castaways Resort & Spa sits on a stretch of sand that is 9 miles long. From the beach right outside your room, you can snorkel out to coral reefs. The resort also offers spa services and whitewater rafting.
